About Live Drafting

Live Drafting is a Hydra-style live-coding environment that sits on top of the AdaCAD weave draft making library.

Edit TypeScript in the overlay editor. Play, pause or step through your sketch and see the result as a fullscreen draft image behind the code. Pause and download a image of the screen as well as a bitmap of the design for your digital loom. Import into AdaCAD to further adjust to generate a threading and tie-up for your dobby or shaft loom.

This has been mostly vibe-coded by Laura Devendorf in Cursor...in less than a day! Mess with it in Github to add new features

Sketches are saved in your browser under livedrafting-sketch. Code runs locally via esbuild-wasm — suitable for creative exploration, not as a security sandbox.

Available functions

The following functions are available in the editor:

  • twill(raised: number, lowered: number, face: boolean)
  • satin(repeat: number, shift: number, facing: boolean)
  • tabby(warps_raised: number, warps_lowered: number, base_pics: number, alt_pics: number)
  • waffle(float_length: number, binding_rows: number, packing_factor: number)
  • random(ends: number, pics: number, pcent: number)
  • interlace(drafts: Draft[], repeats: boolean, weft_oriented: boolean)
  • shift(draft: Draft, shift_ends: number, shift_pics: number)
  • tile(draft: Draft, warp_repeats: number, weft_repeats: number, mode: number, offset: number)
  • addColors(draft: Draft, weft_colors: number[], warp_colors: number[])
  • renderCount()
  • oscillator(min: number, max: number, options?: { frequency?: number; phase?: number })
  • display(draft: Draft)
