Designing with Machine Learning (ML)

This tool was designed to help you maximize your creativity with Ada-CAD!

---

To start designing with machine learning, all you have to do is select your style from the dropdown below and hit "Go!". This will cause your draft to be fed into an ML algorithm which will combine your design with the style you choose and generate suggestions.
