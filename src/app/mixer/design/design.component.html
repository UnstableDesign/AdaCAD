Interaction

Chain Operations

To connect operations together, click the on the bottom of a draft and connect it to the on the top of an operation.

Highlight an Operation Chain

If you'd like to highlight the chain of operations that generated a particular operation input, you can do so by clicking the on the input

Multiselect Operations

Hold the shift key and click all the operations you would like to select. Once you let go, you can move them all as a group.

Add Materials to Drafts

Some operations allow you to add in information about the materials and systems to be used when they compute a new draft. These operations will have a as one of their inputs. The material and system information from the draft you connect will be used in the resulting draft
